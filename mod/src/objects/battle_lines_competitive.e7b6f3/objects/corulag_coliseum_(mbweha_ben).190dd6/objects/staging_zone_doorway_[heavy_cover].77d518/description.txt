Trooper: Impassable
Ground Vehicle: Impassable
Repulsor: Impassable

Height: 1

Doorway is open terrain for troopers.

Troopers (and AT-RTs) can spend 2 actions to clamber over to the otherside. Troopers roll 1 white defence die per mini for falling damage.

AT-STs treat this as difficult terrain.
