Light Cover:
All but AT-ST & T-47

Log:
All - Impassable

LMB+RMB to place underneath. Only models that fit beneath may move under.

Non-emplacement troopers w/ leader in base contact with log may move from one end of the log to the other by using 2 movements, or by using 1 movement & rolling white dice as if clambering.

Units with Unhindered may move across using 1 movement & not roll dice.
